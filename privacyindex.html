Privacy Policy

HabitMood ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you use our mobile application, HabitMood (the "App").

1. What data do we collect?

We collect the following data when you use the App:

  • Email address: We collect your email address when you sign up for an account using Clerk authentication.
  • Name: We collect your name when you sign up for an account using Clerk authentication.
  • User IDs: We collect your unique user ID from Clerk authentication and your RevenueCat app user ID.
  • Purchase history: If you purchase a subscription, we collect a record of your purchases using RevenueCat.
  • Health data: We collect your mood check-in data, sleep hours, and energy levels that you log within the App.

We do not collect any other personal data, such as your location, contacts, or device information.

2. How do we use your data?

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To manage your user account and subscription status
  • To personalize your experience within the App
  • To send you important notifications related to your account
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

We do NOT use your data for advertising, marketing, or sharing with any third parties.

3. How do we protect your data?

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure storage, and access controls.

4. How can you manage your data?

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. To do so, please follow these instructions:

  • To update your email address or name: Log in to your Clerk account and make the changes there. HabitMood will receive the updated information automatically.
  • To delete your account and all associated data: Go to the Settings screen within the HabitMood app and choose "Delete Account". This will erase all your data from our systems.
